Online Classes

(New!) The official site for Online Classes is located here. Over the break, I had more time to reflect on how I learned math and science, and realized that by creating a club to help elementary and middle schoolers during COVID-19, I could help many eager students learn more about mathematics. With other students, I created the Online Classes Club and accompanied by my classmates we reached 117 students in the first Online Classes session (which lasted around a week). The second Online Classes session is taking place now (as of 05/03/20), so join here!
