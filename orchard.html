Mission Statement

Euler's Orchard serves to provide high quality handouts to teach those who are interested in a variety of subjects.

About Us

We are a group of students who enjoy learning and wanted to share that with others. Our name is Euler's Orchard, which is an array of one-dimensional "trees" of unit height planted at the lattice points in one quadrant of a square lattice.
